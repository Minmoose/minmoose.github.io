The upcoming hub for Final Fantasy XIV creativity and community

What is World of Etheirys?

From the Brio dev comes, World of Etheirys an upcoming community platform with the idea of bring together eveyone who love to customize and share their creative content. Whether you're passionate about glamours, poses, mods or Brio, this will be your central hub to discover, share, and celebrate the creativity of the FFXIV community.

Hasn't this been done before?

Sort of! There have been various community efforts in the past to create mod repositories and galleries for FFXIV content, but World of Etheirys aims to be a comprehensive, user-friendly platform. Made with the community, from the very start, not hidden away to one day be a surprise.

This will be a hard project, there is no doubt about that, there will be a lot of opinions, again, this isn't the first time this has tried to be done, but with the help and effort of the community, I think we can make something great together.

What are the features!?

Below you can find a very short list of features I am aiming to implement for launch, the idea here is that this is all based on the community feedback and what you all want to see, so if you all don't want something, I won't build it, but if you do, so shall I do so! With that, please join the Discord and let me know your thoughts and feedback! Hope to see you there! And, Happy Posing!
